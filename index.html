How the game works?

Kings is a character game designed for MacOS, Windows and iOS.

The computer randomly selects a four-character combination to be guessed. Characters are chosen from a set of 4 characters, and a certain character can appear more than once. You have 10 attempts to guess the combination, and after each attempt the computer shows how many signs you guessed right and how many of those signs are in the right 👑 or in the wrong 🌸 position. You have 5000 points and for each wrong move you get negative 496 points.
